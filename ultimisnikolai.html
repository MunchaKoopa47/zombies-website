Nikolai Belinski

(Ultimis)

Nikolai Belinski is a Russian soldier who suffers from substance
abuse. He mentions it all the time during the maps you play on and
also mentions his many marriages. He's also comic relief similar to
Dempsey. He makes many references to Russia.

"I'm warning you, I'm flammable!...Hehehe, pull my finger!"

-Nikolai Belinski
