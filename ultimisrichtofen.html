Edward Richtofen

(Ultimis)

Edward Richtofen is essentially the leader of the ultimis group.
He had created the zombies and some of the weaponry seen in the maps.
Richtofen is a very delusional german scientist. He has mentioned
hearing voices in his head and he yells a lot. He also has a very
eccentric personality.
