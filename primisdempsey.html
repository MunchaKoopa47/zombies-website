Tank Dempsey

(Primis)

Primis Tank Dempsey is a much more tame version of his
ultimis counterpart. Though he has similar quotes and mannerisms
to his Ultimis self, he is much more focused on the mission.
Tank Dempsey also has a distrust oft he Richtofen in this
universe like Ultimis Dempsey. His distrust changes after
Der Eisendrache and he is also very thoughtful to
Takeo and Nikolai.

"Field report, comfirming arrival at site identified in our
intel. No sign of the target nor do the other agents believe
to be on intercept course. Dempsey out."

-Tank Dempsey
