Tank Dempsey

(Ultimis)

Tank Dempsey is an American Marine with a tendency to break the
fourth wall. He tends to be the comic relief of the group and cracks
nonsensical jokes and insults. He has a glaring distrust for Richtofen.
Him and his companions, besides Richtofen, aren't the brightest
individuals.
