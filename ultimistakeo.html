Takeo Masaki

(Ultimis)

Takeo Masaki is a Japanese soldier who fights for the honor of
the Japanese emperor. He makes references to this emperor and
speaks of fighting for honor. Nikolai and Takeo have a brewing
hatred between them. Also, his favorite perk is Quick-Revive
because it tastes like sushi.

"I have no ammo.... I have no honor..."

-Takeo Masaki
