Nikolai Belinski

(Primis)

Primis Nikolai Belinski does not suffer from substance abuse
unlike his Ultimis counterpart. Primis Nikolai is much more rational
and likes his companions more in Dimension 63. He does not trust
Richtofen but eventually becomes more reasonable. Nikolai becomes
leader of the group after Blood of the Dead.

""Whatever has become of my former allies, I must follow guidance
of Kronorium. There is much to do, and much to be undone."

-Nikolai Belinski
