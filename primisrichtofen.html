Edward Richtofen

(Primis)

Richtofen in Dimension 63 is much more reserved than his
Ultimis counterpart. Primis Richtofen seems to know much more
about the magical and scientific aspects of the world and is
still the leader of the group for the majority of the story.
This version of the character is on the side of good.

"I’m so sorry, I really don’t have time to explain.
I have a universe to set right. Thank you, und goodbye."

-Edward Richtofen
