Takeo Masaki

(Primis)

Primis Takeo Masaki is a much more humble version of his
ultimis counterpart. Primis Takeo still speaks of honor and
the emperor and has distrust of the others but he soon changes.
Takeo finds out from his future self that the emperor has
betrayed him, which throws off his whole character.

"Time... can be a cruel mistress. In its relentless march
forward, it robs each of us of many things."

-Takeo Masaki
